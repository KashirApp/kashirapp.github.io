Before You Contact Us

To help us assist you more quickly, please include the following information in your email:

  • Your device model and operating system version (iOS/Android)
  • Kashir app version
  • A detailed description of the issue
  • Steps to reproduce the problem (if applicable)
  • Screenshots or screen recordings (if relevant)

Common Issues

Here are some quick solutions for common problems:

  • App crashes or freezes: Try force-closing the app and reopening it, or restart your device.
  • Sync issues: Check your internet connection and try again.
  • Login problems: Make sure you're using the correct npub (Nostr public key).
  • Feature not working: Make sure you have the latest version of the app from the App Store (iOS) or Google Play Store (Android).
